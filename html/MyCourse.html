Course Overview:

Web Development Fundamentals - CSIS 1280-004 (Fall 2024) by Reza Ghaeli

This Web Development Fundamentals course is designed to give students a strong foundation in building and designing websites from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My goal with this course is to help you gain a practical understanding of each of these languages, so you can create well-structured, visually appealing, and interactive websites. You'll work on everything from the basics of HTML for page structure to CSS for layout and styling and JavaScript for bringing sites to life with dynamic content. Each week, we’ll focus on new concepts like responsive design, multimedia integration, and animations. I’ll guide you through both lectures and hands-on labs to build up your skills progressively. Assignments and a group project will give you the opportunity to apply what you've learned, with regular feedback to support your progress. All course materials, updates, and assignments will be managed through Blackboard, so you’ll have everything in one place. Whether you’re aiming to understand web design for your career or personal projects, this course will give you the essential skills to make it happen.
